Dutch Bros Coffee was founded in 1992 by Dane and Travis Boersma in Grants Pass, Oregon. The company started as a pushcart espresso stand and has grown to become one of the largest drive-through coffee chains in the United States.

Dutch Bros is known for its high energy customer service culture. Employees, called "broistas", are trained to create genuine connections with customers. The company motto is "speed of service with a smile."

The Dutch Bros menu includes a wide variety of drinks including espresso, cold brew, smoothies, freezes, and teas. The signature drink is the Annihilator, which is a chocolate macadamia nut breve. The Golden Eagle is another popular drink made with caramel and vanilla.

Dutch Bros has a loyalty program called Dutch Rewards. Customers earn points on every purchase which can be redeemed for free drinks. The app also allows mobile ordering at participating locations.

Broistas are expected to memorize the full drink menu and be able to make any drink accurately and quickly. Training typically takes two to four weeks. New broistas shadow experienced staff before working the window independently.

Dutch Bros has a strict uniform policy. Broistas wear company branded shirts and are expected to maintain a clean and professional appearance. Hair must be tied back when working with food and beverages.

The company culture emphasizes giving back to the community. Dutch Bros Foundation supports causes related to suicide prevention, muscular dystrophy, and local community needs. Every year Dutch Bros hosts a straw free day donating proceeds to charity.
